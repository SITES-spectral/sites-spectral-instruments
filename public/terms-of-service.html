Our Mission

SITES Spectral is a Thematic Center within the Swedish Infrastructure for Ecosystem Science (SITES). Our mission is to provide support to SITES field stations and fulfill our commitment to FAIR and Open Science principles, following the guidelines of Lund University and SITES.

These terms apply to all users of the Service, including station administrators, researchers, and visitors accessing public data.

2. Service Description

SITES Spectral provides the following services:

  • Instruments Registry: A comprehensive database of spectral instruments deployed across SITES stations
  • Station Support: Technical coordination and support for SITES field stations
  • Calibration Management: Tracking and documentation of instrument calibrations
  • Data Coordination: Facilitating FAIR data practices and open science
  • ROI Management: Region of Interest definitions for phenocam data processing

3. User Accounts and Access

7. Data Integrity

7.1 Legacy Data Protection

To maintain data integrity for time-series analysis (L2/L3 phenocam products), the system implements special protections for ROI (Region of Interest) data. Modifications to existing ROIs create new versions while preserving the original data for historical continuity.

7.2 Backup and Recovery

We maintain regular backups of all data. However, users are encouraged to maintain their own records of critical information.
